Portable reputation

Earned trust that travels with the merchant across channels, rather than residing only within a single platform’s review system.

Full definition

Portable reputation describes the subset of a merchant’s earned trust that exists independently of any single platform’s review or rating system. A merchant with high portable reputation has brand search volume, exportable testimonials, and third-party verification that survives a platform change. A merchant with low portable reputation has trust signals that are entirely platform-resident: if the platform closes the account or the merchant leaves the channel, the reputation does not transfer. Portable reputation is one of the eight dimensions scored by OutMerchant.

Usage note

Portable reputation is written in lowercase mid-sentence: “the merchant’s portable reputation score” not “the merchant’s Portable Reputation score.”
