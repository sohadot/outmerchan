Outmerchant Lexicon

The governed vocabulary for merchant sovereignty and independent commerce. Every term used in the Outmerchant Score and site is defined here with precision. Terms are versioned and traceable to governing decisions.

Core concepts

  • Outmerchant — the brand, the project, and the concept
  • Merchant Sovereignty — the central concept of independent commercial control

Score dimensions

  • Platform Dependency — structural exposure to a single platform’s decisions
  • Platform Capture — loss of pricing and promotional autonomy
  • Route-to-Buyer Control — ownership of the path between product and buyer
  • Portable Reputation — trust evidence that survives platform transitions
  • AI Merchant Sovereignty — positioning for AI-mediated commerce
  • Agentic Commerce Readiness — infrastructure for agent-initiated trade
  • Machine-Readable Trust — trust signals legible to automated systems
  • Governance Independence — self-determined commercial governance

Extended vocabulary

  • Non-Aligned Commerce — trade that does not serve platform interests
  • Settlement Exposure — financial risk from platform-controlled settlement
  • Agent-Selectable Merchant — a merchant readable and selectable by AI agents
