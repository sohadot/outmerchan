Outmerchant

Definition

Outmerchant is the name, the brand, and the concept: a merchant who trades outside the capture logic of dominant platforms — maintaining sovereignty over pricing, buyers, reputation, and governance.

Context

The word combines “out” (outside, beyond, exceeding) with “merchant” (a trader who buys and sells). An outmerchant is not anti-platform; they are post-capture — a merchant who has built enough structural independence that platform decisions do not determine their commercial fate.

Related terms

  • Merchant Sovereignty
  • Platform Dependency
  • Platform Capture
