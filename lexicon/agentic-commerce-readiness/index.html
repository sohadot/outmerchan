Agentic Commerce Readiness

Definition

Agentic Commerce Readiness is the degree to which a merchant’s infrastructure, data, and processes support commerce initiated, mediated, or completed by AI agents acting on behalf of buyers.

Context

Agentic commerce requires machine-readable product data, programmatically accessible pricing and availability, and transaction flows that do not require human intervention at every step. A merchant who is not ready for agentic commerce is not visible to an increasing proportion of buyer intent. Readiness is a spectrum: partial readiness (readable data, no transactional API) is better than none.
