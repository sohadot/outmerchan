AI Merchant Sovereignty

Definition

AI Merchant Sovereignty is the condition in which a merchant maintains control of their commercial identity, product data, and terms of trade in AI-mediated commerce environments — remaining selectable, readable, and authoritative to AI agents and systems.

Context

As AI agents increasingly mediate commerce — searching, comparing, selecting, and transacting on behalf of buyers — merchants whose data is unstructured, whose identity is platform-defined, or whose terms are not machine-readable become invisible. AI Merchant Sovereignty is the specific extension of Merchant Sovereignty into AI-mediated commerce: the ability to participate in AI-driven trade on terms the merchant controls.
