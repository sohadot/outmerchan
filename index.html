How sovereign is your commerce?

OutMerchant scores your structural independence from platform control across eight dimensions. The score tells you where you are exposed, what that exposure costs you, and what moves the number.

Score your business

Eight dimensions of merchant sovereignty

Platform capture is not a single condition; it is a composite of eight structural exposures. OutMerchant measures each one and aggregates them into a single sovereignty score.

  • Platform Dependency — What share of your revenue requires a specific platform to function?
  • Route-to-Buyer Control — Can you reach your buyers without platform mediation?
  • Portable Reputation — Does the trust you have earned travel with you, or stay behind on the platform?
  • Operational Independence — Can you fulfil, pay, and serve without a specific platform’s infrastructure?
  • Data Ownership — Do you hold your own transaction and buyer data, or does it live only inside a platform?
  • Financial Independence — How exposed are your margins and cash flow to unilateral platform fee and payout decisions?
  • Governance Independence — How much of your ability to trade depends on a single platform’s policy decisions?
  • Agentic Readiness — Can AI agents discover, evaluate, and transact with you without human mediation?

Why merchant sovereignty matters now

Platform commerce has created the most efficient distribution infrastructure in history and the most concentrated dependency risk. Merchants who built on platforms in the 2010s found that the rules, fees, and access conditions that made the platform attractive in year one were not the conditions that governed them in year five.

The next transition is already beginning. AI agents are becoming the intermediaries between buyers and merchants. The merchants who are not legible to agents — whose trust signals require human interpretation, whose policies are buried in unstructured text, whose inventory is not machine-readable — will find themselves excluded from agent-mediated commerce in the same way that merchants without online presences were excluded from search-mediated commerce in the 2000s.

OutMerchant exists to make that structural exposure visible before it becomes a crisis.

How OutMerchant works

  1. Score — Answer the structured assessment. Each question maps to one of eight dimensions and is weighted by its structural impact on sovereignty.
  2. Understand — Receive a dimension-by-dimension breakdown with an overall sovereignty level: Captured, Dependent, Resilient, or Sovereign.
  3. Act — Each dimension report includes the specific moves that raise the score, ordered by structural impact.

Key terms

OutMerchant uses precise language. These terms have governed definitions:

  • Merchant sovereignty — the condition of trading without structural dependence on any single platform.
  • Platform capture — the state in which a platform’s unilateral decisions can materially impair or end a merchant’s ability to trade.
  • OutMerchant — the scoring system and the standard it embodies.
