What Outmerchant does

Most independent merchants operate on platforms they do not control. Pricing decisions, visibility, buyer relationships, and dispute resolution are all mediated by companies whose interests are not aligned with the merchants who use them. Outmerchant gives merchants the tools to measure that exposure and reduce it.

The Outmerchant Score

A structured self-assessment across eight dimensions of merchant sovereignty: platform dependency, platform capture, route-to-buyer control, portable reputation, AI merchant sovereignty, agentic commerce readiness, machine-readable trust, and governance independence. The score produces a sovereignty level — Exposed, Dependent, Resilient, or Sovereign — and a dimension-by-dimension breakdown.
